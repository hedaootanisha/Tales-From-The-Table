About This Project

This project was created to help cafe lovers find the most soulful and cozy spots in their city. Whether you're looking for a place to work, relax, or meet up with friends, we guide you to hidden gems with great vibes and even better coffee.

Designed with a passion for aesthetics and simplicity, this site is a curated space built using HTML, CSS, JS.
